Traveling Shows



Bring the Flying Theater Machine out to entertain you! We’ve gone out to schools, Child Care Centers, libraries, festivals, and parties. We love entertaining big groups and our cast is ready to make your event exciting and original.

Because we are an improvised show, you can always ask us to add in themes or elements that work best for your event. We will perform for about 45 minutes and then afterwards the cast will answer questions and mingle with the audience. The total cost for this is $500 for the first show and $300 for subsequent shows – though mileage charges may apply outside of the Austin area.

We are also proud to announce that we are part of the 2014-2016 Texas Touring Roster, which means that there are grants available to help book our shows!

For more information, contact Jessica.
